Welcome to AgriRSLab

Agricultural Remote Sensing Laboratory - INPE

At AgriRS Lab, we develop research focused on producing science and technology based on remote sensing and geoinformatics, aiming to generate both basic and applied knowledge for the agricultural sector. The goal of AgriRS is to develop and improve methodologies that effectively promote the sustainable advancement of Brazilian agricultural activities, contributing to increased productivity and competitiveness. In addition, we are committed to training highly qualified Masters and PhDs within the Remote Sensing Graduate Program at INPE (PGSER). If you would like to know more about our laboratory and our research, please follow us on social media.
